GLH Education – Delete Account & Data Guide

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

GLH Education respects your privacy and gives you full control over your account and personal data. This guide explains how you can request the deletion of your account and associated data.

1. How to Delete Your Account

To delete your GLH Education account:

Log in to your GLH Education account on our website or app.

Send an email to support@glheducation.com
 with the subject:

Delete My GLH Education Account


Include your username or email address associated with your account.

Our team will process your request and confirm receipt within 24–48 hours.

Your account and all associated personal data (profile information, course progress, messages) will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

2. Data Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be removed:

Personal information (name, email, username)

Student progress and activity in courses

Uploaded assignments or files

Messages and forum posts (unless required by law to retain)

3. Data Retention

Some data may be retained temporarily for legal or operational reasons:

Backup logs and records for up to 90 days may be kept before permanent deletion

Data required for compliance with legal obligations (if any) may be retained as required

4. Partial Data Deletion

Currently, GLH Education does not support partial deletion of data without deleting the account. If you wish to remove specific information, contact support@glheducation.com
 and our team will guide you.

5. Contact for Questions

If you have questions about data deletion or privacy policies, contact us:

Email: support@glheducation.com

Website: https://glheducation.com/privacy
